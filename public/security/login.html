Welcome

Welcome to the ng-conf 2016 Lightning Talk application. This application is for attendees only of ng-conf 2016. Here you can both submit to give a lightning talk, and also vote on talks that others have submitted. Talks are anywhere from 2 to 30 minutes long and will be held on Thursday May 5th.

Log in with the email address you used to register for your ng-conf ticket. No password is required. If you need any help, email info@ng-conf.org for support, with the subject "lightning talks".
